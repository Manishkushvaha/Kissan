The Farmer Scheme is a government initiat ive designed to empower and uplift farmers across the nation. Our mission is to provide financial assistance, resources, and support to small and marginal farmers, helping them enhance agricultural productivity and improve their standard of living.

With a focus on sustainable farming practices and rural development, the Farmer Scheme aims to create a positive impact on the agricultural community. We believe in fostering a thriving agricultural sector that contributes to the overall growth of the nation.

Explore our website to learn more about the various components of the scheme, eligibility criteria, and how we are working towards building a stronger and more prosperous farming community.
